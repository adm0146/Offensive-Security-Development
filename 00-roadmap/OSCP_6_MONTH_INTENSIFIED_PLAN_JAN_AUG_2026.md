## üí™ MAINTAINING MOMENTUM

### Spring Semester (Jan-May) Tips
- **Study blocks are NON-NEGOTIABLE** - Schedule OSCP before courses, not after
- **Weekends are OSCP intensive** - Saturday mornings are your biggest time investment
- **Quality over quantity** - 15 focused hours beats 20 distracted hours
- **Progress tracking** - Log each machine, each technique, each "aha moment"
- **Community engagement** - Talk to other OSCP seekers (TryHackMe, Reddit forums)

### Summer Intensive Tips
- **Treat it like a job** - 9-5 OSCP study schedule (10 AM office hour mentality)
- **Deep work sessions** - 4-hour uninterrupted blocks with minimal breaks
- **Writeup while fresh** - Finish machine, immediately document while you remember
- **Rest strategically** - One full day off per week (non-negotiable)
- **Nutrition matters** - Fuel your brain, don't survive on coffee

### Pre-Exam Tips
- **Exam simulation is critical** - Full 24-hour practice run at least once
- **Tool mastery** - Know Metasploit AND manual exploitation equally
- **Documentation practice** - Time yourself writing 3-4 machine writeups
- **Sleep schedule** - Sync to exam time zone/schedule 1-2 weeks before
- **Confidence building** - Easy machines are morale boosters
