## Quick Tips for Exam Day

✓ **Read questions carefully** - One word changes everything
✓ **Understand acronyms** - Half the battle is decoding the question
✓ **Performance-based questions** - Practice these heavily (15-20% of score)
✓ **Time management** - Allocate ~1 min per MC question, 5-10 min per PBQ
✓ **Skip and return** - Don't get stuck on one question
✓ **Trust your preparation** - You've studied the content thoroughly
✓ **Breathe and focus** - Mental clarity beats panicking
